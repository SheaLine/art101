Pseudocode for making guacamole

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Cut the avocadoes in half.
  3. Remove the pits from the avocadoes
  4. Scoop the avocadoes into a bowl.
  5. Mash the avocadoes.
  6. Add the lime juice.
  7. Add chopped cilantro.
  8. Add chopped onions.
  9. Add chopped tomatoes.
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Mix all the ingredients together.
  12. Serve the guacamole with tortilla chips.

Pseudocode for programming Rock, Paper, Scissors

  1. Display game instructions on the screen.
  2. Initialize the Game once the player picks settings.
  3. Collet player input (have the player choose rock paper or scissors.)
  4. Randomly generate the computer input (rock paper, or scissors.)
  5. Determine the winner, check winning conditions.
  6. update the score
  7. Repeat steps 3 - 6 until the player settings are met, (best out of 3, 5, 1)
